Setup guide
Enable in 3 steps
1
Tap the address bar icon in Safari

⎆
Tap the icon in the address bar
2
Select "Manage Extensions"
A menu will appear — tap Manage Extensions to see all your installed extensions.
⇄
Manage Extensions
3
Toggle Tera on
Find Tera in the list and toggle it on. Tap "Always Allow on Every Website" when prompted.
